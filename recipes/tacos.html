Traditional Tacos

Description

Traditional tacos are made with corn tortillas filled with seasoned ground beef, cheese, and lettuce. They are typically served with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, shredded
  • Salsa, sour cream, and guacamole, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, and garlic. Drain off any excess grease.
  2. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for 1 minute more.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Place a spoonful of ground beef mixture in each tortilla.
  5. Top with shredded cheese and lettuce.
  6. Serve with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.
