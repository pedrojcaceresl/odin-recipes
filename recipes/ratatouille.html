Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a classic French dish made with eggplant, zucchini, tomatoes, onions, and garlic. It is typically cooked slowly in a pan until the vegetables are tender and flavorful. Ratatouille can be served as a side dish or as a main course.

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant, peeled and diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the eggplant, zucchini, onion, and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper and cook until the vegetables are tender and the sauce has thickened, about 20 minutes.
  3. Serve hot or at room temperature.
